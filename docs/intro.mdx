# Intro

Welcome! 👋

These are my study notes for the **AWS Certified Developer – Associate (DVA-C02)** certification exam. This documentation is a comprehensive collection of concepts, hands-on labs, and best practices I've compiled while preparing for this certification.

**Note:** These notes are based on the [Ultimate AWS Certified Developer Associate Course](https://www.udemy.com/course/aws-certified-developer-associate-dva-c01/) by **Stephane Maarek**. You may notice his name and references from the course throughout these notes.

## What You'll Find Here

Detailed sections covering AWS Developer Associate topics, including:

- IAM and access management
- EC2 and instance management
- Storage solutions
- Databases and data services
- And much more!
