# Creating an AWS account

In this video Maarek guided me on how to create an AWS account, I signed up for a free tier account.

## Key takeaways

- Visit the [AWS website](https://aws.amazon.com/) and click on the "Create an AWS Account" button.
- Enter a root user email address and account name, then verify the email.
- Create a secure password that meets specific criteria (minimum length, mix of char types).
- User can choose between a free or paid account plan:
  - Free plan: No credit card required, automatically closes if credits run out.
  - Paid plan: Requires credit card for billing and supports production-ready workloads.
- It is recommended to start with a free account for learning purposes.
- Even with a free plan, credit/debit card information needed for identity verification (a A$1 charge made on my case, but it will be refunded).
- Confirm identity via phone number and select a basic (free) support plan.
- After signup, an email confirming account activation will be sent.

  ```txt title="Welcome to AWS - Your account with Free plan is ready"
  Your account is now active and ready to use. Your free plan account includes:
  • USD $100 in AWS credits with opportunities to earn $100 more within the next 6 months
  • 30+ AWS services providing monthly free usage limits (https://aws.amazon.com/free/)
  • No charges within free plan limits unless you explicitly upgrade to a paid plan
  • AWS Basic Support for billing and account assistance

  Important information:
  • Your free plan expires on [6 months from today] or when credits are fully used
  • Your account will automatically upgrade to a paid plan if you commence use of AWS Organizations, or certain other AWS services and offers.
  ```

- Instructions to log into the AWS console include using the root user email and following any prompts.
- Congratulation on successfully creating an AWS account 🎉
